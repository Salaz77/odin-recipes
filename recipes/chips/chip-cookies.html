Chocolate Chip Cookies

Easy homemade cookies to enjoy with your familiy <3

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of butter, softened
  • 1 Cup of white sugar
  • 1 Cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2 Teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 Teaspoons of hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 Cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 Cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 Cup chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 175C. Beat butter, white sugar and brown until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and walnuts.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 5cm apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  8. Let them cooldown.
  9. Enjoy!
