Fresh Cranberry Sauce

Let's make a homemade tasty Cranberry Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of water.
  • 1 Cup of white sugar.
  • 12 Ounces of fresh cranberries.

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Pour water into a saucepan, add the sugar and boil until dissolved (5 minutes).
  3. Add the cranberries, reduce the heat to low and simmer until is chunky (10 minutes).
  4. Pour into a serving dish or jar and cover until ready to use.
